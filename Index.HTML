How to make pizza

How to make the dough:
1- All purpouse flour-280g 2- Water-180ml 3- Yeast-7g 4- Salt-1 teaspoon 5- olive oil-1 tablespoon 6- Sugar-1 teaspoon Pizza sauce ingredients: 1- Tomato sauce or puree-120ml 2- Garlic-1 clove(minced) 3- Herbs-(your choice) 4- sesoning/salt&pepper 5- olive oil-1 teaspoon 6- mozzarella(your choice)

Prepare the dough: 1- In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy. 2- In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. 3- Mix until a dough forms, then knead on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic. 4- Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–1.5 hours or until it doubles in size. Prepare the sauce: 1- In a small saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté garlic until fragrant. 2- Add tomato sauce, oregano, basil, and salt. Simmer for 5–10 minutes, then remove from heat and let cool. Assembele the pizza: 1-Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C), placing a pizza stone or baking sheet inside if available. 2- Punch down the risen dough and roll it into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface. 3- Spread a thin layer of sauce evenly over the dough.
