How to make pizza

How to make the dough:


1- All purpouse flour-280g 2- Water-180ml 3- Yeast-7g 4- Salt-1 teaspoon 5- olive oil-1 tablespoon 6- Sugar-1 teaspoon

Pizza sauce ingredients:


1- Tomato sauce or puree-120ml 2- Garlic-1 clove(minced) 3- Herbs-(your choice) 4- sesoning/salt&pepper 5- olive oil-1 teaspoon 6- mozzarella(your choice)

Prepare the dough:


1- In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy. 2- In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. 3- Mix until a dough forms, then knead on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic. 4- Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–1.5 hours or until it doubles in size.

Prepare the sauce:


1- In a small saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté garlic until fragrant. 2- Add tomato sauce, oregano, basil, and salt. Simmer for 5–10 minutes, then remove from heat and let cool.

Assembele the pizza:


1-Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C), placing a pizza stone or baking sheet inside if available. 2- Punch down the risen dough and roll it into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface. 3- Spread a thin layer of sauce evenly over the dough. 4- Sprinkle cheese over the sauce and add your chosen toppings. 5- Drizzle a little olive oil on top.

Bake:

:
1- Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. 2- Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

Tips:


1- For extra crispy crust, bake on a pizza stone or preheated baking tray. 2- Experiment with different cheeses like cheddar, parmesan, or feta for varied flavor. 3- Use fresh herbs like basil or parsley as a garnish after baking for added aroma and taste.

Following these steps will let you enjoy a warm, homemade pizza with your favorite toppings in the comfort of your home.
